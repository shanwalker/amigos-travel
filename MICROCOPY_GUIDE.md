# Travel Amigo - Microcopy & Messaging Guide

## 🎯 Brand Voice: Playful & Inviting Travel Companion

### Core Principles
1. **Friendly, not formal** - Like chatting with a travel-savvy friend
2. **Clear, not clever** - Avoid confusing idioms or Western-only references
3. **Confident, not pushy** - Guide without pressure
4. **Inclusive** - Works for both Indian and global audiences

---

## 📝 Quiz Introduction Microcopy

### Option 1: Playful & Inviting (RECOMMENDED)
**Headline**: "Ready for an adventure?"
**Sub-text**: "Just answer a few quick questions—share your travel style, interests, and budget—and we'll find your ideal trip!"

- Mirrors Pack Up + Go's successful approach

- Injects excitement and mystery

## 🎨 Quiz Question Microcopy

### Step 1: Personality
**Question**: "What best describes your travel personality?"
**Options**:
- 🏖️ **The Relaxer** - "Beach vibes, spa days, and slow mornings"
- 🗺️ **The Explorer** - "Hidden gems, local experiences, off the beaten path"
- 🎭 **The Culture Seeker** - "Museums, history, art, and local traditions"
- 🌃 **The Night Owl** - "Nightlife, parties, and vibrant city scenes"

**Microcopy tip**: Use emojis for visual appeal, keep descriptions short and evocative

### Step 2: Interests
**Question**: "What are you interested in?"
**Sub-text**: "Select all that apply (choose at least one)"
**Options**: Culture & Heritage 🏛️, Adventure Sports 🏔️, Food & Cuisine 🍜, Nature & Wildlife 🌿, Shopping 🛍️, Nightlife 🎉, Wellness & Spa 🧘, Photography 📸

**Microcopy tip**: Multi-select with clear visual feedback (checkmarks)

### Step 3: Duration
**Question**: "How long would you like to travel?"
**Options**:
- **3-5 Days** - "Quick getaway"
- **5-7 Days** - "Perfect week"
- **7-10 Days** - "Extended trip"
- **10+ Days** - "Epic adventure"

**Microcopy tip**: Add context to help decision-making

### Step 4: Budget (Indian Market)
**Question**: "What's your budget per person?"
**Sub-text**: "We'll find the best options within your range"
**Options**:
- ₹20,000 - ₹40,000 - "Budget-friendly"
- ₹40,000 - ₹70,000 - "Comfortable"
- ₹70,000 - ₹1,00,000 - "Premium"
- ₹1,00,000+ - "Luxury"

**Microcopy tip**: Use ₹ for Indian market, $ for global. Show value, not just price.

### Step 5: Travel Style
**Question**: "How would you like to travel?"
**Options**:
- 🎒 **Solo** - "Just me, myself, and I"
- 💑 **Couple** - "Romantic getaway for two"
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Family** - "Quality time with loved ones"
- 👯 **Friends** - "Squad goals"
- 👥 **Group** - "Join like-minded travelers"

**Microcopy tip**: Relatable descriptions that match user mindset

### Step 6: Branching Logic
**Question**: "How would you like your result?"
**Sub-text**: "Choose your adventure path"

**Option A - Matched Trip**:
- 🎯 **Show me my perfect match**
- "We'll find the best trip that matches your preferences and show you all the details"

**Option B - Surprise Trip**:
- 🎁 **Surprise me!**
- "Keep the destination a mystery until closer to your trip. Trust us to plan something amazing!"

**Option C - Custom Trip**:
- ✨ **I want a custom trip**
- "Work with our travel experts to create a completely personalized itinerary just for you"

## 🌏 Localization Guidelines

### Indian Market Adaptations
1. **Currency**: Always use ₹ (Rupees)
2. **Terminology**: Use "holiday" or "trip" (not "vacation")
3. **Budget Ranges**: Tailored to Indian spending patterns
4. **Social Proof**: Emphasize reviews and testimonials (51% rely on them)
5. **Safety**: Extra emphasis on vetted, safe destinations
6. **Value**: Highlight "best options within your budget"

### Global Market Adaptations
1. **Currency**: Use $ or allow currency selection
2. **Terminology**: Use "vacation" or "trip"
3. **Budget Ranges**: Adjust to Western spending patterns
4. **Examples**: Mix of worldwide destinations
5. **Tone**: Slightly more adventurous, less safety-focused

### Universal Elements (Work for Both)
- ✅ Emoji usage (universally understood)
- ✅ Clear, simple English (no complex idioms)
- ✅ Visual hierarchy (icons, numbers, bullets)
- ✅ Trust signals (testimonials, ratings)
- ✅ Privacy assurance

## 🎯 Error Messages & Edge Cases

### Quiz Validation Errors
- **No selection made**: "Please select an option to continue"
- **Budget too low**: "We'd love to help! For trips under ₹20,000, please contact us directly for budget-friendly options."
- **No dates selected**: "Please select your travel dates or choose 'Flexible' to continue"

### Result Page Edge Cases
- **No match found**: "Looks like you're one of a kind! We'll create a custom trip for you instead." (Redirect to Custom Trip page)
- **Multiple matches**: "Great news! We found 3 perfect trips for you. Here's our top recommendation:"
- **Sold out trip**: "This trip is currently full, but we have similar options available. Would you like to see them?"

### Technical Errors
- **Loading failed**: "Oops, something went wrong loading your result. Please refresh or try again in a moment."
- **Submission failed**: "We couldn't save your answers. Please check your connection and try again."
- **Session expired**: "Your session has expired. Don't worry—we saved your progress! Click here to continue."

## ✅ Microcopy Checklist

Before launching, verify:
- [ ] All CTAs are action-oriented and specific
- [ ] No jargon or complex idioms
- [ ] Currency symbols match target market
- [ ] Trust signals present on every result page
- [ ] Error messages are helpful, not blaming
- [ ] Privacy assurance mentioned
- [ ] Social proof included (testimonials, numbers)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (short, scannable text)
- [ ] Emoji usage is consistent and meaningful
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice throughout
- [ ] All questions have clear value ("why are we asking this?")
- [ ] Progress indicators set expectations
- [ ] Next steps are always clear
